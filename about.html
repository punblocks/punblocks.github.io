My work is characterized by a strong emphasis on people: the individual user experience, needs of the community, and responsiveness to the physical/socio-cultural context. I am particularly interested in the use of adaptive solutions in designing resilient architecture that can meet the changing needs of current and future communities. Generating lasting social value not only contributes towards creating positive, sustainable environments, but also drives economic incentives that attract clients.
